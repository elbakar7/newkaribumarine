## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Deep Ocean Blue:** #002F4B (Primary brand color)
- **Marine Blue:** #004E7C (Accent)
- **Sandy Neutral:** #E8F4F8 (Backgrounds)
- **Gold Accent:** #C9A050 (Highlights)
- **Dark Text:** #001820 (Body text)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (Light text on dark backgrounds)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (Serif) - Elegant, luxury feel
- **Body Text:** Montserrat (Sans-serif) - Clean, modern, readable

- Calm and reassuring
